# Global Machine Design Services — Remote Mechanical Engineering for Industrial Manufacturers
---

## About Global Machine Design Services

Global Machine Design Services (GMD Engineering) is a Vietnam-based remote mechanical engineering firm founded in 2026. The team delivers production-ready machine design, Autodesk Inventor modeling, FEA and motion simulation, conveyor system engineering, lifting equipment design, and complete manufacturing documentation to industrial manufacturers across Europe, North America, Australia, and Asia-Pacific.

All engineering work is delivered remotely. Every project produces a complete, production-ready package, not concept sketches, not reference models. The deliverable your manufacturing team receives is ready to go directly to the shop floor.

The core platform is Autodesk Inventor. The team works in Inventor daily across complex multi-component assemblies, sheet metal, weldments, iLogic design automation, and Vault-managed documentation. Manufacturing drawings are produced to the standard applicable to the client's target market: ISO for Europe, ASME Y14.5 for North America, and AS 1100 for Australia.

## Delivered Project Portfolio

Projects completed by the GMD Engineering team include:

- **VF7 Automotive Front Arm Welding Machine** — Custom robotic welding system for automotive front arm components. Full 3D assembly, fixture engineering, and manufacturing documentation in Autodesk Inventor.
- **Automatic Dimension Measuring Machine** — Inline dimensional inspection equipment for production line quality control.
- **Robotic Bag Palletizing System** — Automated end-of-line palletizing including gripper design, infeed conveyor, and pallet station structure.
- **Function Testing Machine** — Custom end-of-line functional test equipment with sensor integration provisions and complete manufacturing documentation.
- **Bag Arranging and Palletizing System** — Integrated automation combining bag arrangement, orientation correction, and palletizing in a single system.
- **Automated Goods Handling Robot System** — Flexible robotic material handling for industrial logistics, including end-of-arm tooling and support structure design.
